Building SoundCloud

Our story began at SoundCloud with Amir as Mobile Product Manager and Mike as Lead Mobile Designer. We shipped several features on both iPhone and Android applications including a completely rebuilt, redesigned iPhone app. Through our work on the new iPhone app, we aggressively pushed SoundCloud forward as a product for mainstream listeners and completely redefined how people listen to SoundCloud on mobile devices.

What's next

After putting our blood, sweat, and tears into SoundCloud’s mobile apps for two years, we’re taking everything we’ve learned and making an entirely new product.
